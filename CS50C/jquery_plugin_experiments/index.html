Check out the Skeletabs jQuery plugin!

You can use it to create a tabbed navigation bar for your site

This site uses the xzoom jQuery plugin to create zoomable images

Try mousing over the plot to zoom in on a region.

The xzoom plugin also allows you to display zoomable

Try clicking one of the thumbnails to enlarge the image and zoom around. You can also use the scroll wheel to change to magnification amount.

Unfortunately the two plugins don't play very nicely together, so you must always click a gallery image after navigating to a tab for the first time. You can also try refreshing the page after navigating to a new tab to get the image to show up.

Enjoy exploring this data of joint angles and forces put through the right foot during 2 minutes of walking!
